COMP90024 PROJECT2

The Covid-19 analysis in Austrialia

Project introduction



COVID-19 is a highly contagious respiratory disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. The disease has been spreading across the world very quickly and could be fatal. No special treatment was discovered for recovery. In Australia, there are more than 7000 confirmed cases and 100 death cases by now. The disease brought devastating impacts on social, economic and political aspects.

With the outbreak of COVID-19, there is a large growing number of tweets regarding COVID-19. People not only give their opinions, attitudes towards the disease, but also record their daily life under the impacts of the disease. In our project, we make use of these tweets to undertake COVID-19 data analysis in Australia.

Our project aims to build a web-based data analytic system on Unimelb Research Cloud using automatic deployment with ansible. The topic of our project is to investigate people’s attitude towards COVID-19 by analysing twitter data. We harvest COVID-19 related tweets in Australia on the Unimelb Research Cloud and compare these data with the one downloaded from the Australian Urban Research Infrastructure Network (also known as AURIN) for sentiment analysis. Different scenarios were setting up to demonstrate how the people in major cities such as Sydney and Melbourne give attitudes differently from those in other cities. Besides that, factors such as education level or life satisfaction ratio which affect people’s attitude towards COVID-19 were also discussed.
