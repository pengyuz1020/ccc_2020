Sentiment Analysis

Tweets about Covid-19 in Austrialia

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis has been done on the tweet contents to assign an attitude to the tweet which differs from positive, neutral and negative.There's attitude ratios towards COVID-19 for seven cities are plotted along with the High Education rate and High Life Satisfaction rate. For cities,in general, Australia shows a positive attitude towards the disease which may be due to the high stage of medical and biological science development in Australia. Six cities except Darwin follow the pattern that there are more people being positive than negative towards the COVID-19. Adelaide has the highest positive rate among 7 cities which is 40.49% and Darwin has the highest negative rate which is 40.74%.
